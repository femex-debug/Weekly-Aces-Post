ROUND ROBINRules + How to Log Scores11:00 AM
Round Robin Week 2 is live so let us make sure everyone is clear on how this works 🎾 THE MATCH One set. First to 6 games wins. Simple. But if it gets to 5-5 you cannot win 6-5. You have to win by 2. So keep playing until someone gets to 7-4 or better. If it reaches 6-6 you go to a tiebreak. THE TIEBREAK First to 7 points but same rule - you have to win by 2. So 7-6 does not win the tiebreak. Keep going until someone leads by 2 clear points. SCHEDULING YOUR MATCH Go to the leaderboard at myacesclub.com, find your matchups for this week and just text your opponent. Do not wait for them to reach out first. Be the one who makes it happen. LOGGING THE SCORE After you play, go back to the leaderboard, open the actual match and log the score right there on the match. Not on the Submit page. Directly on the match. If the score does not get logged, it does not count toward your group standing. Simple as that 💚 @all
