TIPFemi Tip of the Day10:00 AM
Week 4 and this one is about your head not your hands 💡 Where your eyes go your game goes. Most players look at the net when they miss. Look at where you WANT the ball to go instead. Pick a spot on the other side of the court before you swing then trust your body to hit it there. Sounds too simple to work but I promise you it changes everything about how you play under pressure. Try it tonight. Pick your spot first 🎾 - Femi

TIPFemi Tip of the Day10:00 AM
Let us talk about the return of serve 💡 Most people just react when the ball comes at them. Instead try this: before the point starts decide if you are going to go crosscourt or down the line. Make the decision BEFORE the serve happens. Then commit to it. You do not have to be right every time but having a plan makes your return cleaner and your brain calmer. Stop reacting. Start deciding 🎾 - Femi

TIPFemi Tip of the Day10:00 AM
Something nobody talks about enough 💡 The tension in your racket affects everything about how your ball behaves. Loose strings give you more power and spin but less control. Tight strings give you more control but less pop. If you have been playing with the same strings for over a year they are probably dead and you are basically playing with a wet blanket. Get your racket restrung. It is one of the cheapest upgrades you can make and the difference will shock you. Ask me tonight and I will point you in the right direction 🎾 - Femi

TIPFemi Tip of the Day10:00 AM
Thursday tip and this one is for everybody 💡 Stop apologizing when you win a point. I see it all the time. Someone hits a great shot, the ball clips the tape and falls over, and the person who hit it goes "oh sorry that was lucky." No. You hit the shot. Own it.
