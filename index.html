Doubles tip for the tournament 💡 Communication is everything in doubles. Before every point the serving team should have a quick plan. Is the net player poaching? Is the server coming in behind the serve? Even just a nod between partners makes a huge difference. The teams that talk to each other on the court are almost always the teams that win 🎾 - ACES
